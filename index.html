About Me

Healing Through Movement & Care

I am a dedicated physiotherapist passionate about restoring function and improving quality of life. With clinical training across neurological, orthopedic, geriatric, and cardiothoracic domains, I bring a holistic, evidence-based approach to each patient.


My journey began at SSG Hospital under The Maharaja Sayajirao University, where I developed strong foundations across multiple rehabilitation disciplines. I now serve as a Consultant Physiotherapist at Zainee Hospital, Vadodara, specializing in neuro rehabilitation — helping patients with stroke, Parkinsonism, and ataxia regain independence and dignity.


Beyond clinical work, I continuously invest in advanced skills — from Dry Needling to Manual Therapy and IASTM — to deliver the most effective, modern care possible.

Core Competencies

Clinical Skills & Expertise

Manual Therapy
Hands-on mobilization and manipulation techniques to restore joint mobility and reduce pain. Certified by CAPRI Institute.
Electrotherapy
Proficient in IFT, TENS, and Ultrasound Therapy modalities for pain management and accelerated tissue recovery.
Neurological Rehabilitation
Specialized treatment of stroke, Parkinsonism, ataxia, and other neurological conditions to restore functional movement.
Exercise Therapy
Individualized strengthening, stretching, and mobility programs tailored to each patient's recovery goals.
Pediatric Physiotherapy
Neuro-pediatric rehabilitation supporting children's motor development and functional recovery.
Dry Needling
Certified dry needling practitioner targeting myofascial trigger points for effective pain relief and muscle recovery.
Orthopedic Rehabilitation
Treatment of musculoskeletal injuries, post-surgical recovery, and chronic orthopedic conditions.
Geriatric Physiotherapy
Age-specific rehabilitation addressing mobility issues, fall prevention, and quality of life for elderly patients.
IASTM
Instrument Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization to address fascial restrictions and chronic soft tissue dysfunction.
